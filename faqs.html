Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and initiatives.

Program Related Questions

We offer several programs including:
  • Information Technology Excellence Program (ITEP)
  • Soft Skills Training
  • English Communication
  • Placement Support
Each program is designed to provide comprehensive training in technical and professional skills.

Our main IT Excellence Program is 6 months long, with classes running Monday through Friday. Other programs vary in duration from 1 to 3 months, depending on the course content and intensity.

Yes, all our programs are completely free for eligible students. We believe in making quality education accessible to underprivileged youth who have the potential but lack resources.

Admission Related Questions

Basic eligibility criteria include:
  • Minimum 12th pass in any stream
  • Age between 18-25 years
  • Family income below ₹3 lakhs per annum
  • Basic English communication skills
  • Strong willingness to learn

You can apply through any of these methods:
  • Fill the online application form on our website
  • Visit our center personally
  • Call our admission helpline
After application, shortlisted candidates will be called for an entrance test and interview.

Placement Related Questions

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement support including:
  • Resume building
  • Interview preparation
  • Mock interviews
  • Campus placements
  • Direct company referrals

Our students typically receive packages ranging from ₹3-6 LPA, depending on their performance and the hiring company. The average package is around ₹4.5 LPA.

Donation Related Questions

You can donate through multiple channels:
  • Online payment through our website
  • Direct bank transfer
  • Cheque or DD
  • UPI payment
All donations are eligible for tax exemption under 80G.

Donations are used for:
  • Student training and development
  • Infrastructure maintenance
  • Teaching resources
  • Student support services
We maintain complete transparency in fund utilization and share regular updates with donors.
